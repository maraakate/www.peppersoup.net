Arukotchi translated!



1. First, remove the tab from the back of the case
2. Press the "all reset" button.
3. With that, the opening scene will play on the screen and then the clock set screen will appear. Use the N button to select the units you want to change (hours or minutes) and use the Y button to change the time.
* If you've entered the wrong time, or you simply need to reset the time after you've started the game, simply go to the clock screen and press the Y and N buttons simultaneously for about 2 seconds to return to clock set mode.



START
Arukotchi has started walking! If you don't walk with her, none of the following will happen. As you walk, her health rating will gradually increase.
Item Find!
There will be times when Arukotchi finds an item while walking. Items will raise her beauty rating. She may also come across a mystery box [you won't know what's inside] and she will ask you if she should open it or not.
Meeting a gotchi-boy
Arukotchi will also meet gotchi-boys as she walks! It seems that Arukotchi's fitness and beauty rating affect which gotchi-boys she can meet.
Receiving a gift from a gotchi boy
Gotchi-boys with whom Arukotchi has met and given her name to will bring her presents.

Gotchi-boy proposes...

If Arukotchi accepts the gotchi-boys gifts, his "love-love rating" will increase and finally, one night, he will propose.
