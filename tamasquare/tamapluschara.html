Tamagotchi Plus Characters
The following information was translated from both the official Bandai Tamaplus Site and the Bandai Tamaplus Raising Guide (the title in Japanese is "Kaettekita Tamagotchi Plus Suku Suku Sodatekata Guide").

Marutchi
Rebellious Age		 About every 1.5 hours

Empties 1 heart about every 45 minutes

Empties 1 heart about every hour

Intelligence: Level 1
Strength: Muscle 1
Base Weight: 20g
Still a baby personality, and very inquisitive. It enjoys rolling from side to side.
Will appear the first time you play, and in subsequent generations will be born to parents that do not have high discipline levels.
   
Kinakomotchi
Rebellious Age
Takes pride in its skin.		 About every 1.5 hours

Empties 1 heart about every 45 minutes

Empties 1 heart about every hour

Intelligence: Level 1
Strength: Muscle 1
Base Weight: 20g
Is a jealous type, and when he gets overexcited, his head gets inflated.
Will only appear from the second generation on, to parents that both have high discpline levels.

**Kinkomochi is a type of Japanese dessert - mochi (pounded rice cake) rolled in soybean powder. You can see a picture here The reference to his jealousy is part of a play on words - "yakimochi" is roasted rice cake, but can also mean 'jealous person' or personality. Mochi, when roasted, also has a tendency to puff up, hence the inflated head.
