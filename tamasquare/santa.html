Santaclautchi Translated!

1. Before you raise a Santaclautchi

[1] Remove the battery tab.
With a beep, Santa's house will appear

[2] Reset the unit.
Using a thin object, press the reset button on the back. You will hear a beep.
*When pushing the reset switch, do not press too hard.

[3] Set the date and time
First, set the date. Press the B button to bring up the date adjust screen. With the A button select the month and with the B button, select the day. Press C to finish.
Next, set the time. Press the B button again and this time the time adjust screen will appear. Use the A button to select the hour and the B button to select the minutes. Press C to finish.
Finally, press the B button to take you back to the screen showing Santa's house. (Please see section 9 for further information on the date/time adjust screen)

[4] In about 5 minutes, Santaclautchi will appear!
* If you do not set the date and time, Santaclautchi will not appear
* Until Santaclautchi does appear, you cannot use any of the functions except for the date/time adjust and reset button.

2. Santaclautchi arrives!

When Santaclautchi's attention icon lights up and he beeps, it means he wants something. Use the 'needs' icons to give him what he wants. Be sure to give him what he asks for or Santaclautchi will get mad and return to his house!When you need to give him something...

A button -> to cycle through the icons B button -> to select C button -> to cancel

When you want to know what Santaclautchi is asking for, have a look at his 'check meter'.

3. The 'needs' icons

* When Santaclautchi gets mad and returns to his house, you cannot use any of the icons.

Check Meter

Use the A button to select the icon and use the B button to open the Check Meter. From here you can use the A button to see the 'Status' and 'Stock' screens. Within the 'Status' and 'Stock' sections, use the B button to see Santaclautchi's condition. Use the C button to return to the Santaclautchi screen.

[Status] Press the A button to see Santaclautchi's current condition. Use the B button to cycle through each 'status' screen.
Age/Weight
Here you can check on Santa's growth. If Santa's weight increases too much, he will become shy and easily intimidated so be careful about it. To get him to lose weight, play the Chimney Game.

Hunger/Happiness
There are 4 hearts for each indicator. When all the hearts are black, Santaclautchi is content. When he is hungry, he'll activate his attention icon to ask for food.



Santarashisa -> {Could be translated as Santa-ness, Santa-osity... basically, how much like Santa is he}
In the beginning this meter will be empty. As Santaclautchi gets each of his 4 main items (please see section 4), each bell will get filled. If you increase his Santarashisa, maybe something good might happen...

Distance
This meter shows how far Santaclautchi is from all his tamagotchi friends who are waiting for him. If you fill the bar, you've reached your goal!

[Stock] Use the A or B button to see how much meat pie and cake Santaclautchi has in his stocks. He can only have up to 4 of each at a time.
