There will times when you will have to scold or pet your tama. Learning when to use the appropriate action will help you raise a good, well behaved character.
when to scold: when the food and happiness meters are full, but it is still calling you (call sign is lit)
when to pet: if it cries during the infancy or rebellious stages - if it is turning its back to you (insecure screen) even though the food and happiness meters are filled
