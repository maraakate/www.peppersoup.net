How to play with My Sapling
1. Viewing the modes:
Pressing the select button will cycle through the different modes.

2. Selecting a mode:
Touch the touch sensors to enter the mode that is displayed on the screen.
*If you do not enter a mode within 30 seconds, it will automatically go to Communication Mode

Communication Mode
When not using the other modes, the unit will normally be in Communication Mode.

* If there is no input from the Select Button or Touch Sensors within 1 minute, the curtain will close and the unit will go to standby.

Let's look at the growth and change animations!
Fulfilling basic conditions will allow you to view the growth and change animations.
1. Touch the touch sensors once. (the sensor will acknowledge you with a beep)
2. If you have fulfilled the basic conditions, fanfare will be played and the growth/change animation will begin.
*If the conditions have not been met, nothing will happen


*"change" indicates a change in the way you handle your 'My Sapling', resulting in a different type of sapling, therefore there is no growth.

Let's Communicate!
Saplings love to play! Play with your sapling a lot so it can grow.
1. Touch the touch sensors twice. (you will be acknowledged by two beeps)
2. A melody will play.
3. The light and sound dome will flash red, green or blue three times (while beeping).
*it may be difficult to see the lights if you play the game outside or in a brightly lit room
4. After the 3 flashes, you must repeat the pattern by using the action which corresponds to the colour:
Red Light : Let the unit hear some sort of noise, such as talking
Green Light : Shake the unit gently, to let it feel motion
Blue Light : Bring the unit close to light, so it can sense brightness
5. If you get the sequence correct, you sill see your sapling smile.
If you get something wrong, it will become angry. If you don't do something, it will begin to cry.

The absent sapling
If you do not play with your sapling often enough, it will become bored and run off somewhere. Try different approaches to get your sapling to come back.
1. If you haven't played with your sapling for a while, touching the touch sensors once will bring up a screen like the one shown to the right.
*There are different types of 'out' screens!
2. Try making noise, putting it under lights, gently shaking it, pressing the buttons, touching the sensors, many times to get your sapling to come back.
*do not press the reset button!


3. Even if you see your sapling, it may be in a bad mood and unwilling to return right away. A screen like the one to the right will appear, and you will have to wait a little while. *there are different types of this screen as well!

Silent Mode
How to turn the sound on and off

1. From the mode menu, enter silent mode.
2. Touch the touch sensors once.
3. Touch the sensors once more and the sound will go off.
4. Touching the sensors will turn the sound on and off.
*if there is no input within 30 seconds, it will return to communication mode
5. Press the select button to return to the mode menu screens.





Light and Sound Mode
Sapling will respond to you with a variety of sounds and lights.
At first it will only flash quickly, but as it grows, it will glow more and more. Your sapling will light up according to how it feels, so it will look different every day.
1. From the mode menu, enter the Light and Sound mode.
2. Touch the touch sensors once.
*if there is no input within 30 seconds, it will return to communication mode
3. Your sapling will respond with sounds and light.
*it may be difficult to see the lights outdoors or in bright rooms
4. Return to the mode menu screens.

Sensor Mode
Saplings will respond to sound, light and movement by showing you a variety of actions.
1. From the mode menu, enter the sensor mode
2. Touch the touch sensors once
3. Let your sapling experience some noise, light, or movement.
*if there is no input within 30 seconds, it will return to communication mode
For sounds, you can talk to your sapling, or play music for it.
For light, try putting your sapling close to a light source.
For movement, try gently shaking your sapling.
4. Press the select button to return to the mode menu screens.

My Sapling Q & A
Q. Nothing is showing on the screen.
A. Check the batteries.

Q. Growth never seems to happen.
A. It seems that playing many times during a short period of time is better for growth than playing often over a long period of time.

Q. How are Growth and Change different?
A. Growth means getting bigger and that you are one step closer to the 'My Sapling' stage. Change means that your sapling has turned into a different type.

Q. I can't get sensor mode to work well.
A. If you are having difficulties with sensor mode, try changing your methods of sensor input or try it in a different environment.
